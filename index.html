About Us

PEAL Sound Enterprise Ltd is a private company limited by shares, based in Nigeria established since 2022 to provide high-quality technological equipments, tools, and services, as well as professional, scientific and technical support services.For more details.

Mission

To deliver top-notch technological equipment and services, professional support, and consultancy services that enhance the productivity and efficiency of our clients in the health, agro-allied, and various other sectors.

Vision

To Cultivate a sustainable and health-centered future by integrating agriculture with innovative health solutions, empowering communities to thrive through nutritious, natural products, and holistic wellness.
